About The Organization

About Us

THE ROSE FOUNDATION is a non-religious organization open to all deprived women especially widows living in Ghana.

Non-Government Organization in Ghana working to the principles held by Madam Rose Yeboah of blessed memory, to tirelessly to support the poor and needy elderly women. We mobilize resources in the form of money, food,

drugs, clothing amongst others and distribute them to older deprived women.We work in partnership with the Presbyterian Church in KuKurantumi, a town in Ghana, where our beloved Madam Rose Yeboah Asante worshipped. We bring hope to those women who live in rancour and are normally chastised in the society they live in.

Our Mission

To work for older persons, especially the poor and marginalized women with special emphasis on widows in upholding their rights while respecting cultural and religious diversity, promoting good health, nurturing their hopes of living in harmony and peace.

Our Vision

Envisions a society where all older persons live in dignity; remain healthy, and secure with a roof over their heads and free from the Insults and innuendos meted on them by the society in which they thrive.

Our Goals

To advance the well-being of older persons through networking and partnership building with various stakeholders at the local, national, regional, and international levels.

To build a sustainable platform that will support the transformation of the lives of deprived elderly women (especially widows) by providing for their urgent needs.

To foster hope for the deprived elderly women towards a longer well fulfilled live!
